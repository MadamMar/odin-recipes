Chicken Fried Rice

A delicious edition to any Chinese meal, or even as a standalone meal! Chicken Fried Rice is a classic.

This recipe is very customizable, add anything you want.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Water
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbso Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 cups White Rice, cooked
  • 2 tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 cup Chopped Chicken, cooked
  • Optional: Any Additional Vegetables

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, beat eggs with water
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over med-low heat. Add egg and leave flat for 1-2 mins
  3. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds, set aside
  4. Heat oil in the same skillet, add onion and saute until soft
  5. Add rice, soy sauce, pepper and chicken (and any additional veg)
  6. Stir-fry together for approx. 5 mins, then stir in the egg
