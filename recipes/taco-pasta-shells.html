Taco Pasta Shells

A Delicious meal for any Taco and Pasta enthusiast!

This meal combines two delicious meals into one. The tasty beef of a taco with the embrace of pasta. The fusion made complete with a delicious sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Jumbo Shells
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 cup Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Cheddar, shredded
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack, shredded
  • 1 Jalapeno, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Large Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 28oz can Crushed Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 300Â°F
  2. In a large pot, boil water and cook the pasta until al dente
  3. Cook the meat with ground cumin and chili powder
  4. Drain the fat from the meat and return to heat
  5. Stir in tomatoes and some cheese (reserve enough cheese for topping)
  6. Cook until cheese is melted, stirring often. Add salt and pepper
  7. In a new pan on medium heat, add olive oil. When heated, add onion and cook until tender
  8. Add garlic and jalapeno, cook until fragrant (approx. 1 min)
  9. Add crushed tomatoes and season with salt and pepper
  10. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 mins, stir in cilantro
  11. Spoon small layer of sauce onto casserole dish
  12. Stuff mixture into the pasta shells
  13. Cover with remaining cheese and sauce
  14. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly
