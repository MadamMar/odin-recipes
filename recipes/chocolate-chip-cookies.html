Chocolate Chip Cookies

One of the tastiest desserts around, especially with a cold glass of milk.

This is a very easy to follow standard recipe for a delicious, melt in your mouth cookie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 cup Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 2 cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat over to 325Â°F. Grease baking sheet or line with parchment paper
  2. Sift together flour, baking soda and salt; set aside
  3. In a medium bowl, cream together melted butter, brown sugar and white sugar until well blended. Beat in vanilla, egg and egg yolk until light and creamy
  4. Mix in sifted ingredients until just blended
  5. Stir in chocolate chips using a wooden spoon
  6. Drop dough by 1/4 cup at a time onto the prepped baking sheets, roughly 3 inches apart
  7. Bake for 15-17 mins, or until edges are lightly browned
  8. Let cool
